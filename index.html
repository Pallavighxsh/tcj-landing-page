PHI Learning's academic journal, The Continuum, is a new educational resource which aims to uplift Indian and Global research. The interdisciplinary journal features both science and humanities subjects. At its nascent stage, we expect the launch of issue #1 soon!


Our Standard Operating Procedure

  • When you enquire, we will send you a form called the "Manuscript Information Report", to be filled out by you.
  • The Journal processing teams include the Editorial Board, Subject Experts, and Reviewers.
  • Manuscript must be submitted in LaTeX or Word Format following the Journal’s style guide.
  • All manuscripts undergo double-blind peer review to ensure unbiased evaluation.
  • You will benefit from end-to-end editorial and production support throughout the publication process.

6 Steps to Get Published
‘The Continuum’, PHI Learning’s Academic Journal

1

Submit your research papers to us via email for initial assessment by our editorial team.

2

Senior editors evaluate the submission for relevance, originality, and adherence to journal guidelines.

3

Manuscripts undergo plagiarism screening and a double-blind peer review where experts assess research depth and novelty.

4

Authors receive feedback, make revisions, and resubmit for final evaluation, if required.

5

Upon approval, the manuscript moves to copyediting and formatting before publication.

6

Your research is published online and in print, indexed for academic accessibility, and promoted through PHI Learning’s networks.
